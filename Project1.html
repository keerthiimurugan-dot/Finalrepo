Srinath

MY SOFT PLACE

Loving you feels like the world finally lowers its voice.

With you, everything slows down — my thoughts, my fears, my breathing.

You don’t rush my heart. You let it arrive.

You feel like late nights, dim lights, and music playing low.

Like something meant to be felt, not explained.

I don’t love you loudly. I love you deeply.

In the way I stay.

In the way I listen.

In the way my heart settles when it’s you.

You are not chaos. You are calm.

You are not noise. You are home.

And I choose you — in every quiet version of me.

ALWAYS YOURS
